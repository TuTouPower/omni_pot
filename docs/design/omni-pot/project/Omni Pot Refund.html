This Refund Policy explains how refund requests are handled for purchases of Omni Pot.

1Paddle Purchases

Purchases of Omni Pot may be processed by Paddle. For transactions processed by Paddle, Paddle may act as the reseller, merchant of record, payment processor, or tax handling provider for your purchase, depending on your location and the checkout flow.

Refund requests for Paddle transactions should be submitted through Paddle’s buyer support flow, the purchase receipt email, or Paddle’s order support page.

You may also contact us at support@zzzkkkccc.site for technical support or refund-related questions. However, Paddle may handle the actual refund processing for Paddle transactions.

2Refund Window

You may request a refund within 14 days of purchase if a technical issue prevents you from using Omni Pot substantially as described and we are unable to resolve the issue within a reasonable time.

Examples of eligible technical issues may include:

  • you cannot activate or access the Software after a valid purchase;
  • the Software cannot start on a supported Windows, macOS, or Linux environment;
  • a core paid feature does not function substantially as described;
  • a serious compatibility issue prevents normal use and no reasonable workaround is available;
  • you were charged incorrectly;
  • a refund is required by applicable law.

We may ask for reasonable information to verify the purchase and troubleshoot the issue before approving or recommending a refund.

3Non-Refundable Situations

Unless required by applicable law, refunds may be refused if:

  • the refund request is made more than 14 days after purchase;
  • the Software functions substantially as described;
  • you changed your mind after purchase;
  • you purchased the wrong product or plan;
  • you no longer need the Software;
  • you failed to review the product description, supported platforms, or feature limitations before purchase;
  • the issue is caused by your device, operating system configuration, network, firewall, permissions, third-party software, or unsupported environment;
  • the issue is caused by a third-party translation, OCR, dictionary, text-to-speech, AI, or cloud service outside our control;
  • the issue is caused by third-party service downtime, rate limits, API changes, account restrictions, pricing changes, or provider policies;
  • you violated the Terms of Service;
  • there is evidence of fraud, abuse, unauthorized license sharing, chargeback misuse, or repeated refund requests.

4One-Time Purchases

If Omni Pot is sold as a one-time license, approved refunds may result in deactivation of the license key and loss of access to paid features.

Unless required by applicable law, one-time purchases are not refundable after the 14-day refund window.

5Subscriptions

If Omni Pot is sold as a subscription, you may cancel your subscription to stop future renewals.

Cancellation does not automatically refund past payments. Subscription payments may be refunded within 14 days only if a qualifying technical issue prevents you from using the Software substantially as described and we are unable to resolve the issue within a reasonable time, or if a refund is required by applicable law.

After cancellation, you may continue to access paid features until the end of the current billing period unless otherwise stated during checkout or by Paddle.

6Third-Party Service Limitations

Omni Pot can integrate with third-party services, including online translation, OCR, dictionary, text-to-speech, AI, and cloud providers.

Some online translation features may use services such as Google Translate and Bing Translate by default. These services are provided by third parties and may change, limit, block, degrade, or discontinue access at any time.

We cannot guarantee that third-party services will always be available, accurate, affordable, compatible, or unchanged. Issues caused solely by third-party service availability, API changes, provider restrictions, rate limits, account status, network blocking, or provider policies may not qualify for a refund unless required by applicable law.

7How to Request a Refund

For Paddle purchases, please use one of the following methods:

  1. use the refund or order support link in your Paddle receipt email;
  2. visit Paddle’s buyer or order support page;
  3. contact us at support@zzzkkkccc.site.

Please include:

  • your purchase email address;
  • Paddle order ID or receipt information;
  • operating system and version;
  • Omni Pot version;
  • a description of the technical issue;
  • screenshots, logs, or error messages if helpful.

Do not send passwords, private API keys, sensitive documents, or confidential screenshots unless necessary for support.

8Troubleshooting Before Refund

Because refunds are generally provided for unresolved technical issues, we may first attempt to help you fix the issue.

This may include asking you to:

  • update Omni Pot;
  • restart the app or device;
  • check system permissions;
  • verify network access;
  • test another translation or OCR service;
  • provide logs or screenshots;
  • reinstall the Software;
  • try a reasonable workaround.

If the issue is confirmed and cannot be resolved within a reasonable time, we may approve or recommend a refund through the applicable payment process.

9Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically returned to the original payment method.

Processing times may vary depending on Paddle, your payment provider, card issuer, bank, country, and payment method.

Taxes, currency conversion, bank fees, or payment provider fees may be handled according to Paddle’s policies and applicable law.

10Chargebacks

Please contact Paddle or us before initiating a chargeback.

Chargebacks may delay resolution and may result in suspension or deactivation of your license while the dispute is investigated.

11Legal Rights

This Refund Policy does not limit any mandatory rights you may have under applicable law, including consumer protection, defective digital product, or other statutory rights that cannot be excluded by contract.
